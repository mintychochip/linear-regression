Goal: fit  ŷ = βx + α
by minimizing SSE — no calculus, no matrices.

Methodology: Log-Zoom Grid Search

1
Wide range — start with β ∈ [−5000, 5000] and α ∈ [−100000, 100000].
2
Log-spaced sampling — candidates spaced on a log scale so precision is maintained across all magnitudes.
3
Find minimum SSE — evaluate each (β, α) pair at the current step size and keep the best.
4
Zoom in 10× — shrink the search window around the best point and repeat.
✓
Stop when both β and α are stable to 3 decimal places between iterations.
